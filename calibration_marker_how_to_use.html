Use this mode for a fast, accurate, contactless measurement of planar wounds.

  1. Place a Calibration Marker next to the wound.
  2. Ensure optimal visibility and lighting for the entire wound.
    • Maintain uniform lighting, avoiding harsh shadows, highlights, and overexposure.
  3. Hold your device parallel to the marker and close enough to the wound, ensuring the wound occupies at least 1/3 of the screen.
    • The app will notify you when the device is not parallel to the marker.
    • For smaller wounds, maintain a closer distance to the wound.
  4. Capture the photo.
